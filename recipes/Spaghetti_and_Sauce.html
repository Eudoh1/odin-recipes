Spaghetti and Sauce Recipes

Descriptions

A classic, simple pasta dish with a rich tomato sauce, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano/basil, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Drain spaghetti and add to the sauce; toss to combine.
  6. Serve hot, optionally garnished with grated cheese or fresh herbs.
